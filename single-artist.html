Neha Kakkar

Neha Kakkar is an Indian singer. She competed on season 2 of the television reality show Indian Idol in 2006. After Indian Idol, she kicked off the journey in the music industry with the album ‘Neha The Rock Star’ composed by Meet Brothers in 2008. In the modern era, she is the rising singer in the music industry of India. In 2013, Neha Kakkar gave her voice in the song ‘Dhating Naach’ for the movie ‘Phata Poster Nikhla Hero’.
